Helium Balloon Tips

1

Balloon Preparation

Stretch balloons gently before inflating for best results and longer float time.

2

Proper Inflation

Don't over-inflate balloons. Leave room for expansion, especially in warm conditions.

3

Float Duration

Latex balloons float 8-12 hours, foil balloons can float for days when properly inflated.

4

Storage

Store helium canisters upright in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
